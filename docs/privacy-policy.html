Privacy Policy

OFAC Compliance Search Chrome Extension

Effective Date: December 18, 2025

Last Updated: December 18, 2025

Summary: OFAC Compliance Search does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, addresses, or any customer information you search.
  • We do not collect browsing history or usage analytics.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.
  • We do not require account creation or login.

2. Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • OFAC SDN List Cache: A cached copy of the publicly available OFAC Specially Designated Nationals list to enable offline searching and improve performance.
  • Search History: A local log of your recent searches for your own convenience. This data never leaves your device.
  • Form Auto-Save: Temporarily saved form inputs to prevent data loss if the popup closes accidentally.

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by removing the extension or using the "Clear All" buttons within the extension.

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted to external servers, your information is as secure as your device itself. We recommend following standard security practices such as keeping your browser updated.

6. Children's Privacy

This extension is intended for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.
